# Knowledge Packet - music-example-album-blurb

## Task
- output_type: album_review_blurb
- audience: general music readers
- domain: music

## Non-negotiables
- Claims allowed: concise electronic pop release, sparse percussion, texture-forward arrangement
- Claims forbidden: popularity, influence, chart position, artist biography, production credits
- Uncertainty: thin evidence; keep the blurb modest

## Facts
- The demo album is a fictional electronic pop release.
- Arrangement notes emphasize sparse percussion and surface texture.
- No reception or biographical evidence is available.

## Template
1. Name the listening impression.
2. Describe arrangement texture without inventing credits.
3. Close with a caveat-friendly evaluation.

## Style
Specific, compact, and restrained. Avoid canon claims and cultural overreach.
