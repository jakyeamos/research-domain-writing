# Knowledge Packet — basketball-acceptance-bounded-projection

## Task
- output_type: future_projection
- audience: analytics-literate basketball readers
- domain: basketball

## Non-negotiables
- Claims allowed: the current Jokić profile values, listed center role, bounded playmaking interpretation, and the packet's explicit projection limits.
- Claims forbidden: a numeric forecast, a ceiling or floor value, an injury or availability claim, or a complete future team-value conclusion.
- Uncertainty phrases required: exact sample window is not expanded, cannot establish, evidence is insufficient for a forecast.

- The profile values capture current profile scoring, rebounding, and assist volume.
- The metrics miss efficiency, role difficulty, opponent quality, lineup context, and the exact season/game window needed for a forecast.

## Jargon guardrails
- Use ceiling, floor, dependency, and uncertainty band as unanswered fields, not invented values.
- A bounded projection may conclude that the packet cannot support a projection.
- Keep “current profile” separate from “future performance.”

## Template structure
1. State the current evidence.
2. Identify the missing projection drivers.
3. Give ceiling/floor status as not established.
4. End with a responsible non-forecast.

## Style
Calm and explicit. A refusal to overstate evidence is the useful conclusion.
