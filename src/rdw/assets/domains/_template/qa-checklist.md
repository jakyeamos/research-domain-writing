# Domain QA Checklist (Template)

Use after domain copywriter, before humanizer/blader.

## Grounding

- [ ] Every factual claim appears in the knowledge packet or research packet
- [ ] No invented statistics, dates, names, or citations
- [ ] Uncertainty language present where confidence is low/medium on contested points
- [ ] Interpretation labeled as interpretation in source material

## Jargon & terminology

- [ ] Terms match domain definitions in concept/jargon bank
- [ ] No decorative jargon (terms used without evidentiary tie)
- [ ] Forbidden generic phrases absent

## Argument quality

- [ ] Conclusions follow from cited evidence
- [ ] No unsupported comparisons
- [ ] Caveats included for small samples, role changes, missing data

## Audience & format

- [ ] Matches requested output_type and audience
- [ ] Appropriate depth for research tier

## Humanizer handoff

- [ ] Draft is complete enough that humanizer need not add facts
- [ ] Qualifiers must survive humanization

## Severity guide

| Severity | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| blocker | invented stat, false citation, wrong entity |
| major | overclaim, wrong jargon, dropped uncertainty |
| minor | vague phrase, rhythm only |
