# Concept: Production as argument

**Domain:** music  
**Slug:** production-as-argument

## Definition

In criticism, production is not background — it's part of the album's thesis. Mix choices, sampling, arrangement density, and vocal treatment are evidence for claims about intent, era, and audience.

## When to use

- Album reviews where sound design carries meaning
- Explaining why a record feels "of its time" or deliberately retro

## When not to use

- When credits/production facts are unknown — mark unknown
- As vague praise ("polished production") without observable detail

## Writing guidance

- Tie adjectives to audible specifics: drum sound, sample grain, vocal stack, silence
