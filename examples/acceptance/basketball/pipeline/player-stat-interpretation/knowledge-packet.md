## Metrics
- Points per game captures box-score scoring volume averaged over games played; it misses efficiency, shot difficulty, defensive value, and lineup context.
- Rebounds per game captures box-score rebounding volume averaged over games played; it misses rebound chances, box-outs, transition effects, and defensive scheme.
- Assists per game captures recorded assist volume averaged over games played; it misses potential assists, passing difficulty, turnovers, and creation burden.
