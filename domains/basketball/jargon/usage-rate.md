# Jargon: Usage rate (USG%)

**Domain:** basketball  
**Term:** Usage rate  
**Also called:** USG%, usage

## Meaning (domain-specific)

Percentage of team possessions used by a player while on floor (field goals, free throws, turnovers attributed in standard formulas). Measures involvement, not quality.

## Precision notes

- Not the same as: touch rate, time of possession
- Implies: offensive load; does not imply efficiency or winning impact

## Acceptable usage

- "His 28% usage is second on the team, but the true shooting is only league-average for that volume."

## Avoid

- "High usage proves he's the best offensive player."

## Pair with

- Concepts: usage-illusion
- Metrics: TS%, AST%, TOV%
